WIN CONDITIONS:

JACKPOT! Card = Prediction = Dice (×10 bet)
WIN Card matches Dice (×2 bet)
BREAK EVEN Card matches Prediction (get bet back)
LOSE No matches (lose bet)

DOUBLE OR NOTHING: Available after any win!

ROUNDS: 3 rounds per game

TIP: More cards = harder to match!
